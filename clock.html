VWAP + trap notes
VWAP defines WHERE. Trap defines WHEN. Use 2σ and 3σ bands. Keep the trap anchor alive until 50 minutes after the next execute window. Example: 4:30 trap → 5:00 execute → next trap 7:30 / execute 8:00 → keep 4:30 VWAP alive until 8:50. Do not fade the first ±3σ touch. Require touch → reaction → failure → entry. During 7:30–10:30 and 1:30–4:30, use the dynamic range engine as confluence context. Between 1:30 AM–4:30 AM only trade if 2σ or 3σ, regime aligned, and news present. Otherwise skip.
