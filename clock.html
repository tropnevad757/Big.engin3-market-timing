News Math

Symbol news / symbol news = Value
−/− = + buy
+/+ = − sell
−/+ = − sell
+/+/− = + buy
−/−/+ = − sell

When it’s 2 or more of the same signs and 1 of the opposite sign it becomes weighted.
+/+/− = +
−/−/+ = −

When it’s 2 or more of the same positive and negative signs it becomes negative:
+/+/−/− = −
−/−/+/+ = −
